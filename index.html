Who We Are

The Bao
To Me Story.

Where Heritage Meets Heart. What started as a delivery only kitchen in 2021 has grown into something deeply personal: a celebration of the flavors that shaped our chef's childhood and the culinary traditions passed down through generations.

At Bao To Me, every dish tells a story. Our handcrafted baozi are born from treasured family recipes perfected by Chef Wan Ming Li: a reimagination of his father's recipe for the modern palate.

We're a tribute to the rich tapestry of Pan-Asian cuisine — from the subtle artistry of Chinese and Japanese kitchens, to the bold vibrant soul of Southeast Asian Street food.

Founded by three friends — a techie-turned-chef, a corporate foodie, and a business expert — who turned their love for food, culture, and their city into something greater than the sum of its parts.

"It's not just about the bao — it's about bringing people together."

Our interiors mirror the vibrant energy of Southeast Asian alleyways: layered, lived-in and richly textured. Metal shutters, lightboxes, and vintage fans repurposed into lamps evoke raw authenticity and nostalgic charm.

Welcome to Bao To Me. Welcome Home.
